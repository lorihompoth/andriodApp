# andriodApp
NevaForget

The app that lets you revise what you already know, 
so you won't forget what you had learnt the day after the exam!

Add your questions and answers to the database, start a quiz anytime. 
Sync your your devices to your account to have the quiz available 
everywhere. Customize the questions, upload your own pictures and charts, 
map them to words to later practice blind maps and more...

Organise the questions into topics, customize further the quizzes choosing 
specific tags or let the app randomize them for you.

Follow your results on your statistics, share them to your friends.
